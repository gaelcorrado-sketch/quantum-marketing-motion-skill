# Motion Calibration

Quick defaults for polished motion:

1. Hover/focus feedback:
   - `150-220ms`, ease out
2. Panel/card transitions:
   - `220-320ms`, ease in-out
3. Hero or section ambient loop:
   - one loop max per section
   - low opacity and low amplitude
4. Stagger:
   - `40-80ms` between items
5. Performance guardrail:
   - avoid heavy blur + giant shadows + many concurrent animations on mobile
